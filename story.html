THE AGENCY

A Call to Action

Solving communication challenges is what we do. We thrive on it. We love it. We embrace it with both arms knowing our reputation depends on it. Simply put, it’s our call to action.

Not surprising since that’s what you’d expect from a full–service advertising agency. We’ve built our reputation on putting great ideas to work–powerfully, effectively, memorably, and creatively. Not to mention our reputation as persistent, responsive, confident, and nice. At least that’s what our clients tell us.

Result: challenges met, goals reached, clients satisfied. Happiness abounds.

Located in the National Capital Region, we provide the full spectrum of marketing and advertising services–all the things you’d find o Madison Avenue–plus an expertise in event planning and management. Did we mention workforce development?

Impressive? We think so. Bad ass? Talk to our clients.

ADVERTISING

ADVERTISING

Breakthrough creative that cuts through the clutter. Ads placed in today’s most relevant and performing media. Inventive strategies to surpass your goals.

Good advertising, like good art, has an emotional impact. Great advertising has a practical impact as well. It isn’t enough for advertising to be entertaining–though that’s always good. The goal of advertising is to elicit a desired response, be it making a purchase, taking an action, or changing an opinion. Bring on a great TV commercial, a compelling billboard, a knock–em–dead print campaign. That’s our forte.

We believe that advertising is measured by results, not awards. Of course, we covet recognition from our peers as much as anyone, but we are in the business of driving results for our clients. Our experience and expertise tell a pretty compelling story about our success at creating success for our clients.

Advertising. We love this stuff.

BRANDING AND IDENTITY

RESEARCH & STRATEGIC PLANNING

RESEARCH & STRATEGIC PLANNING

Market research is intended to answer many questions. Hence the name research. But often the hardest part of doing research is figuring out which questions to ask.

MarCom Group’s team of experienced researchers and strategists rely on a discovery process to collect information by identifying the right questions—the best questions–and selecting the appropriate research tools that provide solid and reliable data.

Primary or secondary sources. Quantitative or qualitative approach. Focus groups. One–on–one interviews. Online and phone surveys. A–B testing. It’s a pretty impressive toolbox that informs really impressive strategic plans.

It’s simple: develop a research plan, select the research tools, execute the plan, and evaluate the results. Then put the results into action. Can you say awesome strategies?

MEDIA PLANNING & BUYING

A wise man once said that the greatest sin an advertisement can commit is to go unnoticed. While this is usually a statement associated with how creative an ad might be, it really doesn’t matter how eye–catching and provocative an ad is if it placed where target audience doesn’t see it.

The media landscape is ever changing. New mediums. Existing vehicles repurposed. The upstart digital option that just popped up. Old reliables that just keep on ticking. We’ve got our finger on the media pulse and understand what’s working now.

Understanding the media consumption patterns of target audiences allows our team of planners and buyers to execute media campaigns that are timely and cost–effective, but also reach the audience at times and in environments which make them most receptive.

WEBSITES/DIGITAL ENGAGEMENT

WEBSITES/DIGITAL ENGAGEMENT

Tweet them to the punch.

A website is really a price–of–entry item in today’s marketplace. But if it looks like price pf entry than it’s probably doomed to underperformance and maybe failure.

We’ve done some very impressive work putting our client’s websites on the digital map. Sure, we loved the days when Flash made it easy to make web animations sizzle. We remember when e–commerce was just about the only thing discussed when a website project came our way. Now we rely on new tools that haven’t diminished the sizzle and certainly haven’t impeded functionality. If anything, websites are better than ever.

Websites have moved beyond the “online brochure” purpose to allow companies and organizations
to build engaging relationships with consumers, clients, prospects, and employees to name a few.
Today’s website does all that and can be visited and experienced from just about anywhere and
on devises that didn’t even exist a few years ago.

Our team of UX/UI designers, developers, and system engineers can execute a modern, full functioning, and responsive website for just about any purpose and for any user. From the sexiest animations to most practical of landing pages, we make websites that do exactly what it needs to do.

And then there’s website metrics. Yep, you guessed it, we’re experts. Tools and tracking mechanisms built directly into the code to make evaluating performance a breeze.

EVENT PLANNING AND MANAGEMENT

EVENT PLANNING AND MANAGEMENT

MarCom Group started in 1996 as an event management company. Go figure. And we continue to offer
state–of–the–art services for planning and executing meetings and events, large or small.

From conferences to trade shows, we know what it takes to manage all the details for a successful gathering.
We handle it all: venue selection, conference marketing or invitations, exhibit sales, vendor relations, accommodations, catering, entertainment, sponsorships.

We even assist clients in identifying and selecting events they should attend and
support them in their planning and execution.

When you need to bring a group of people together for a common purpose, we’ll make it a memorable event.
Event management is still our bread and butter.

CUSTOM JOB FAIRS

Sometimes you don’t want to be a part of the crowd. While there are plenty of job fairs in the market an
employer’s need is large enough or specific enough to merit an event of its own. When that need arises, MarCom Group provides an integrated package of services that will plan, promote, and execute a custom event. We coordinate all pre–event planning including formulation of an event strategy, as well as orchestrating all the logistics of the on-site event.

We also manage event promotion, ensuring a solid turnout of highly qualified candidates, and help to manage the event underway to ensure smooth sailing. Finally, we help with event follow–up, whether follow–up contacts with attendees or providing reports and analysis of the event’s success.

VIRTUAL EVENTS

Not all events need to be in–person. MarCom Group provides full–service management
of virtual events for any organization.

First, we obtain event requirements and then recommend a virtual event solution: either an off–the–shelf approach, a reputable third–party provider, or our own proprietary Virtual Event Campus (VEC). Our VEC is a
turn–key system that is customizable and can be used for hiring events, conferences, educational seminars—just about anything event can be done virtually with the VEC.

The VEC features live or recorded panel discussions, full video capabilities, live chat functionality, information downloads, surveys, and many other highly flexible and valuable tools.

Whatever the preferred solution, we perform all needed branding and graphic design, event
advertisement, event set–up, training of event staff, live event management, close-down, participant
follow-up opportunities, and reporting.

EXHIBIT DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

An event exhibit has the potential to make or break participation at a trade show, convention, or job fair. Whether a table–top display or a 1,500 square foot mega structure, its design and functionality are vital to making the right impression and a lasting impact.

MarCom Group’s event exhibit design specialists have unique insight into making any organization stand out in a crowd while integrating seamlessly with the rest of its communication campaign. Our experience is long and deep and enable us to respond with unexpected and creative design options—both large and small—that reinforce and build brand awareness.

We also train organization event staff to get the most out of the exhibit and features.
